How We Work

Every project is guided by a clear, structured process shaped by decades of workshop experience. From the initial conversation through to installation, each stage is carefully managed to provide clarity, consistent quality, and dependable delivery. Our approach is thoughtful and practical, balancing design intent with sound construction, durability, and long-term performance.

From Concept to Installation

We begin by listening carefully and understanding both the space and the brief. This includes assessing proportions, layout, and how the area will be used day to day. From there, we develop a clear, considered design direction that balances function, materials, and detail, ensuring the outcome is appropriate to the way the space is intended to work.


Throughout this stage, we collaborate closely with clients, designers, and architects, refining the concept until it is practical, buildable, and aligned with both the aesthetic intent and the realities of long-term use and durability.


Timeline: 1–2 hours | Free, no obligation

We prepare detailed drawings and material selections tailored to your project, allowing the design to be clearly visualised and assessed before manufacture begins. Every element is considered, from proportions and finishes through to construction details and integration with surrounding spaces.


The design is refined in consultation with you until all details are resolved, ensuring the final proposal is accurate, buildable, and clearly documented so expectations are understood by everyone involved.


Timeline: 1–2 weeks | Includes revisions

Once the design is confirmed, we provide a comprehensive quotation covering materials, labour, and installation. Our pricing is transparent and clearly structured so you understand exactly what is included, with no hidden costs or unexpected variations.


We take the time to review the quotation with you, answering any questions and confirming scope, timelines, and expectations so the project can proceed with clarity and confidence.


Timeline: 3–5 days | Fixed pricing

Your project is manufactured in our workshop using proven construction methods, carefully selected materials, and precise detailing. Each piece is built with attention to accuracy, durability, and finish, ensuring it performs as intended and maintains its quality over time.


Throughout the build process, we focus on consistency and quality control, checking fit, function, and finish at each stage. We keep you informed of progress and welcome workshop visits so you can see the work taking shape.


Timeline: 6–12 weeks (varies by project)

We coordinate delivery and installation with care, ensuring each piece is fitted accurately and finished as intended. The process is managed to minimise disruption, with attention given to surrounding spaces and a clean, considered handover on completion.


Our team works methodically on site, coordinating with other trades where required and carrying out a final walkthrough to confirm everything is complete. We remain available for follow-up support to ensure the finished work integrates seamlessly and performs as expected over time.


Timeline: 1–3 days | Followed by final walkthrough
