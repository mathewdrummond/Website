Part of Hawke’s Bay since 1941, Millbrook Furniture Solutions has been creating beautiful, durable furniture tailored to your style.

Millbrook Furniture is a long-established joinery and furniture workshop based in Hastings, with a reputation built over more than five decades of high-quality work. We collaborate with leading architects, designers and businesses across New Zealand to deliver bespoke furniture and interior joinery for residential and commercial environments.


Our team of cabinetmakers brings deep trade knowledge and a shared commitment to precision, durability and craftsmanship. Using proven traditional techniques alongside carefully selected timbers and materials, we create furniture and joinery designed to perform and endure.


With over 90 years of combined trade experience within a close-knit team, we place equal importance on process and outcome. From initial engagement through to installation and ongoing support, we provide a professional, considered experience at every stage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you typically use?

We work with a wide range of materials including solid timbers, veneers, laminates, and complementary finishes such as stone or metal. Material selection is guided by the project requirements, desired aesthetic, and long-term performance.  

Do you manage installation as part of the project?

Yes. Installation is carried out by our team to ensure each piece is fitted accurately and finished to the intended standard. Care is taken to protect surrounding areas and ensure a clean, considered handover.

Can you work from an existing design or architectural plans?

Yes. We regularly work from architect or designer-supplied drawings and specifications. Where required, we also provide technical input to ensure the design is buildable, durable, and resolved to the level expected of custom joinery.

Can you create one-off or highly specialised pieces?

Absolutely. We regularly undertake unique and commissioned furniture projects that require a tailored approach. These pieces are designed and built individually, with close attention to detail, material selection, and execution.

How long does a custom furniture or joinery project take?

Timeframes vary depending on the complexity and scale of the project. Once the scope is confirmed, we provide a clear indication of lead times, including manufacture and installation, and keep clients informed throughout the process.
